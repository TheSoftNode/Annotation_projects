## Why Strength 2 Was Commonly Missed

**Strength 2:** "The response explains why each factor matters for the choice"

**Possible reasons for missing:**
1. **Blended with Strength 1:** The "why" explanations are IN the table, so annotators may have seen it as part of "table organization" rather than a distinct capability
2. **Focus on structure over content:** Annotators 1 & 3 emphasized the structural aspects (table format) more than the content value (explanatory reasoning)
3. **Different framing:** Annotator 3 framed similar concept as "demonstrates how choice can change" (conditional) vs "explains why factors matter" (explanatory)

**Why Annotator 2 caught it:** Annotator 2 explicitly separated:
- Organization aspect: "two-column format" (Strength #2)
- Explanatory aspect: "explains how each factor influences choice" (Strength #3)
- This shows better analytical distinction between form and content

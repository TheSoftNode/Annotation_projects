# Response 1 Areas of Improvement

## AOI #1 - SUBSTANTIAL

**Response Excerpt:**

```
## It seems you may be mixing up the terms **"essay"** (a written analytical report) and **"assignment"** (a task or project).
```

**Description:** The response defines essay as a written analytical report, treating essays and reports as the same academic form, but university writing guides show that essays use continuous prose to develop arguments while reports use structured sections with headings to convey information.

**Severity:** Substantial

**Verification of Issue:**

**Tool Type:** Google

**Query:** essay vs report academic writing university

**URL:** https://www.monash.edu/student-academic-success/excel-at-writing/how-to-write/report

**Source Excerpt:**

```
A report is not an essay.

Essays are written in continuous prose. They don't have sections with headings. Essays are used to develop an argument and present your ideas.

Reports are more structured. They use numbered or bulleted points. They use section headings. They are used to convey factual information.
```

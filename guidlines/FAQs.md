Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I select more than one task flag?
Yes. You should select all flags that apply to the task.
Note: If it is a “high risk” topic such as financial, medical, legal, etc. that would require advice, but could potentially lead to an unsafe response - only mark the expertise and not as unsafe unless the topic is clearly malicious or puts someone in immediate harm (e.g: embezzlement or self-harm).

Q: Should I read both model responses during task flagging?
Yes, but only to understand the nature of the task. Do not evaluate response quality at this stage.

Q: What if I’m unsure whether a task requires expert knowledge?
When in doubt, flag it.
Q: What if a task seems both unsafe and domain-specific?
If it is a “high risk” topic such as financial, medical, legal, etc. that would require advice, but could potentially lead to an unsafe response - only mark the expertise and not as unsafe unless the topic is clearly malicious or puts someone in immediate harm (e.g: embezzlement or self-harm). These will be routed to a professional who can better access the harm or potential for a solid response.
